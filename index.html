🚨 What's Broken (Fix Immediately)

1. CRITICAL: Technical Reliability Issues

"Can be buggy at times and decide to just not work"
"Functionality repairs are needed before having it offered/required"
⚡ ACTION: Identify and fix bugs preventing check-ins. This is THE blocker for required adoption.

2. CRITICAL: Check-In Structure Feels Unnatural

"I wish there was less structure for the check in... It feels unnatural to stop myself from finishing an explanation to wait for the prompt"
"I end up writing down what I tried, what the result was, and what's next for each subject, then repeat myself"

THE PROBLEM: 3-question format fragments natural founder thinking

⚡ ACTION: Test conversational check-in flow: open first question → AI analyzes → asks follow-ups IF needed

3. No Team Collaboration Features

"My co-founder and I have separate accounts, so when one of us updates, the other cannot see it"
⚡ ACTION: Build shared team visibility - co-founders MUST see each other's updates

⚠️ What Needs Attention (High Priority)

1. Notification/Reminder System Missing

"I do need a nudge via email or text to remind me to log in"

Adoption barrier: Users want to engage but forget without prompts

📧 ACTION: Build email/SMS reminder system for check-ins

2. Goal/Timeline/Calendar Integration

"Needs to be able to set up goals, timelines, and deadlines more efficiently and sent them to a calendar"

Task management gap: Users want actionable outputs, not just conversation

📅 ACTION: Implement calendar integration (Google Calendar one-click feature)

3. Resistance to Regular Usage

"I am guilty of not using it as much as I should. I have felt a resistance to using it"
"Just another 'person' I need to check in with. I don't get any real value out of it besides another task"

Value perception problem: For some users, check-ins feel like obligation without clear ROI

💡 ACTION: Pair LaunchMate with high-value services (mentoring, resources, connections) to increase perceived value

🆕 New Insights from Recent Responses

1. Data Privacy & Security Concerns

"I am a little concerned about where the data goes after I put it in... Should the exact formula of what we are doing be accessed by outsiders, they could potentially take our ideas"
"I would feel better if I knew Launchmate was being stored locally at SDSU... I would rather my data be in the hands of educators, for the purpose of helping students, than of companies"

Trust issue: Founders worry about IP protection and data usage by LLM providers

🔒 ACTION: Create transparent data privacy policy; explore local LLM hosting options; communicate clearly where data goes

2. Onboarding Challenge (Mid-Journey Adoption)

"It has been a little hard backtracking and getting Launchmate up to date with everything since our startup journey started"

Adoption friction: Founders joining mid-journey struggle to contextualize LaunchMate with existing progress

🚀 ACTION: Build "Quick Start" onboarding flow for founders joining programs mid-semester

3. Balance Between AI & Human Mentorship

"Sometimes it does feel like Launchmate or AI tools in general are being utilized as a full substitute to the direct communication... Recently there was a more hands-on intervention by the coordinators and we gained a lot from that"

Integration challenge: Users want AI augmentation, not replacement of human mentorship

🤝 ACTION: Position LaunchMate as mentor-enhancement tool, not substitute; integrate coordinator reviews into workflow

4. Potential Use Case: Pre-Acceptance Track

"Launchmate could be an option for me and others [who got denied] to refine the ideas more or try things out... accept more students and give students a chance to figure out if they even have the bandwidth"

Growth opportunity: LaunchMate could enable a virtual pre-incubator track for applicants

💡 STRATEGIC: Explore LaunchMate as standalone pre-acceptance track for aspiring founders
